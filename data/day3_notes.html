The monad of the day is <p>.

In terms of model specification, what we have for contentious terms is basically a case definition: 

phosphorus "John" = "Morning Star"
phosphorus _ = "Venus"
Here we are saying that if the perspective used to interpret the name Phosphorus is John's then the interpretation is "Morning Star", then we use underscore _ to capture all other perspectives. Of course we could have a more varied definition: 
phosphorus "John" = "Morning Star"
phosphorus "Tom" = "Little green men planet"
phosphorus _ = "Venus"
